Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Carew Tarcy ("we," "us," or "our"), a sole proprietorship based in Drummondville, Quebec, Canada, collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the GENA mobile application ("GENA" or the "App"). GENA is a premium fitness application designed exclusively for women that combines real-time spatial body mapping with advanced AI-driven biomechanical analysis to generate personalized insights and workout protocols.

By using GENA, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

1.3 Spatial Body Mapping Data

GENA uses Apple's ARKit framework — the same augmented reality technology powering iPhone's advanced spatial features — to perform real-time body mapping directly on your device. Your iPhone's motion sensors, depth sensors, and TrueDepth camera system capture and analyze your body's spatial positioning across multiple anatomical reference points to generate precise biomechanical measurements.

When you complete a body mapping session, we collect and process:

  • Two photographs of your body (front and back views), captured through the App using your device camera
  • Biomechanical measurements calculated on-device through Apple's ARKit spatial analysis framework, including:
    • Anterior pelvic tilt angle
    • Bilateral asymmetry percentage
    • Volumetric waist-to-hip ratio
    • Cervical deviation measurement
    • Torso-to-femur ratio
    • Dynamic knee valgus level
    • Additional structural alignment data captured from multiple anatomical reference points

Because ARKit processes sensor data locally on your device, the raw spatial sensor data never leaves your phone. Only the final calculated measurements are transmitted to our servers.

1.4 Important — How We Handle Your Body Photos

We do not permanently store your original body photographs.

Here is exactly what happens when you complete a body mapping session:

  • Your photos are captured in the App and temporarily transmitted to our secure servers for processing
  • Your photos are processed through our proprietary AI analysis pipeline to generate your clinical skeleton visualization, genetic map, and biomechanical analysis
  • Once the rendered results are complete, your original photos are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers
  • We retain ONLY the rendered skeleton visualizations (clinical anatomical renderings — not photographs of you) and your biomechanical measurements

Your real body photographs exist on our servers only for the brief period required to generate your results — typically less than 60 seconds.

1.5 Future You — Predictive Transformation Technology

GENA's Future You feature uses advanced AI predictive modeling to project your potential physical transformation over time.

Here is how the technology works:

  • You provide a selfie through the App
  • Your selfie is combined with your personalized workout protocol, biomechanical data from your body mapping session, your fitness goals, and your workout schedule
  • Our proprietary AI modeling pipeline uses this composite dataset to render predictive transformation imagery across three timeframes: 3 months, 6 months, and 12 months of consistent training
  • The model projects body composition changes, muscle development patterns, postural improvements, and structural alignment adjustments expected to occur based on your specific protocol and biomechanics
  • The resulting imagery consists of AI-rendered predictive visualizations — they are not photographs of you and are not guaranteed outcomes

We retain the rendered transformation imagery. Your original selfie is handled with the same privacy principles as body mapping photos — processed briefly for rendering, then permanently deleted from our servers.

Important: Future You imagery consists of predictive visualizations based on statistical models. Actual results depend on many individual factors including consistency, nutrition, genetics, lifestyle, and health. Future You is a motivational and planning tool and should not be interpreted as a guaranteed outcome.

1.6 Recalibration — 30-Day Progression Tracking

GENA's Recalibration feature allows you to rescan your body every 30 days to track real biomechanical progress over time.

Here is how Recalibration works:

  • After 30 days, your Recalibration unlocks within the App
  • You complete a new body mapping session using the same ARKit spatial analysis process
  • Our systems compute precise deltas between your previous baseline measurements and your new measurements across all biomechanical dimensions
  • Your previous skeleton visualization and your new skeleton visualization are presented side-by-side for comparison
  • A new personalized workout protocol is generated based on your updated body data
  • Your new measurements become your rolling baseline, and the cycle repeats

All Recalibration data follows the same privacy principles as your initial body mapping session. Original photos are deleted after processing. Only rendered skeleton visualizations and measurements are retained.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored on servers located in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. Limited AI processing occurs on secure cloud infrastructure during image rendering. Spatial sensor data is processed entirely on-device and never transmitted.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Database access is restricted through Row-Level Security policies — you can only access your own data
  • Authentication is handled by Apple and Google using industry-standard protocols
  • Rendered visualizations are stored in private, access-controlled storage
  • Original body photographs are deleted immediately after processing
  • Sensor data is processed on-device and never leaves your phone

4.3 Data Retention

  • Original body photos and selfies: Deleted immediately after AI processing completes (seconds to minutes)
  • Rendered skeleton visualizations and biomechanical data: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Future You transformation imagery: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Recalibration history: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Workout history: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Account data: Retained as long as your account is active

When you delete your account, all of your data is permanently deleted from our systems, including rendered visualizations, Future You imagery, Recalibration history, biomechanical measurements, workout history, onboarding profile, and account information.
